Frequently Asked Questions

How does JMC operate?

Unlike other competitors, JMC does not have a tutoring center. JMC tutors students in the comfort of their own homes through our online classrooms.

Will I be charged extra or hidden fees?

Absolutely not! Our rates are straightforward, and you only pay for the time you need.

Do you have a tutorial for your learning programs?

If a client is utilizing our services for the first time, our tutors will provide a quick and easy tutorial to help clients get started absolutely free. We also provide customer service in case difficulties arise or clients need help navigating the system.
