How we teach:

Traditional study of syntax and typical "Hello World's" are always a must. However, when it comes to programming, one of the best ways of learning is by doing! Alongside your tutor, you have options to build some simple applications that give you experience utilizing the technologies that will help you understand the broader picture of the language you are trying to learn.
